What is this site?

This is a site I created for my Web Authoring class in college. The basic idea of this site is to re-explore the idea of mathematical functions.

Why functions?

I used to be a high school math teacher, and one of my favorite topics to teach about was functions. I find functions to be both extremely fascinating to try to understand and extremely useful for understanding the world around us.

So what should I do?

If you're interested in learning about functions (whether for the first time or as a refresher), click on one of the chapter titles above. I'd recommend starting with Chapter 1: Function Basics. However, as soon as you feel comfortable with the content in the first chapter, feel free to explore the other two chapters in any order you want!
